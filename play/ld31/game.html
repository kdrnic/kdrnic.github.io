Story:
------
You are a brave warrior from a gallic village,
currently under attack by the forces of the Roman Empire.
Your village has been able to defend itself so far
due to the magic strength potion made by the village's druid,
however, he is running out of ingredients. Help him!

-------------
Use arrow keys to walk
Press Z to attack
